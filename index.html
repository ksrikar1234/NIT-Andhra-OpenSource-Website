Introduction

"I am a lazy person, which is why I like open source, for other people to do work for me."
-Linus Torvalds

Open-Source Software denotes software for which the original source code is made freely available and may be redistributed and modified.In general, open source refers to any program whose source code is made available for use or modification as users or other developers see fit. Open source software is usually developed as a public collaboration and made freely available.

Our Goal

- To make the students of NIT Andhra Pradesh aware of Open-Source Platform.

- To reduce the gap between theoretical and practical knowledge. We keep learning different programming languages but how often do we look into its practical aspect?

- To create a community of "like-minded" students and put in sincere efforts to contribute to open-source.
